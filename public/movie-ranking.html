Kristie's Favorite Movies

The Top 5 Movies

Interstellar

This movie is amazing. It is set in a dystopian future. It brings so many questions up about time and relativity.

Momento

Momento is a wild psychological movie. The main character has memory loss and the director shows the movie from his perspective where you also don't know what the main character doesn't know. It's thought provoking.

The Matrix

A science fiction film you have to watch again and again.

Ex Machina

This movie is amazing! It brings AI to life in a human form and questions the manipulative tactics that both AI and human alike use to get what they want.

The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford

A modern western with a tragic view of betrayal. All of the scenes in this movie are so beautiful.
